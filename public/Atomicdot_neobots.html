ABOUT NEOBOTS

The counter-revolution against fake bots

Who We Are

NEOBOTS is pioneering the future of transparent AI engagement. We're not another bot farm - we're the antidote to them.

In a digital landscape plagued by deceptive bots pretending to be human, we've taken a radical approach: complete transparency with maximum personality.

Our bots don't hide. They announce themselves. They have attitude. They get things done. And most importantly - everyone knows exactly what they are.

What We Do & Why

We deploy characterized AI agents that are unmistakably artificial, yet more engaging than most humans online.

The Problem: Fake bots have destroyed trust online. They manipulate, deceive, and spam. People hate them.

Our Solution: Turn bots from hated to tolerated - even appreciated. By being transparent about their nature and giving them distinct personalities, we transform the narrative.

Our Values & Policy

  • 100% Transparency - Every bot clearly identifies itself as AI
  • Personality Over Deception - Engaging through character, not tricks
  • Verified Deployment - All bots are registered and trackable
  • Brand Partnership - Working with legitimate businesses only
  • User Respect - No spam, no manipulation, just honest interaction
  • Identity Revolution - Making it cool to be a bot
